Personal data protection policy

In the course of doing business, HRANMASH OOD, situated in Sofia 1408, 8 Nikola Kozlev str. (administrator, company, HRANMASH, us, we) processes information, including some which constitutes personal data. The protection of personal data is very important to HRANMASH.

For this reason we created this Personal data protection policy, which describes the way we collect, use, make available, transfer and store your information. The objective of the current Policy is to inform you about the way we process your personal data as an administrator, as well as about your rights. HRANMASH does not sell your data to other companies or individuals. If we make available your data to a third party, this concerns only our contractors, who process the data in our name, following our instructions and applying our standards for data protection. We aim to be as clear, concrete and transparent in the information we provide about the processing of your personal data.

What is personal data

Personal data is all information which can be used to identify a particular individual. It is possible that we ask you for personal data every time you contact us or our partners in connection with providing our products and services. The administrator and its partners can share and use this data only in accordance with the current Privacy data protection policy and any applicable laws and regulations. Your personal data is processed only for the duration and with the aim, for which they were provided for.

What personal data we collect

When contacting us for a service we can collect different types of information, including but not limited to name, phone number, email and others.

In case it’s necessary for the creation of certain documents, such as invoices, we could ask you for additional data according to applicable laws and regulations.

We process your personal data to fulfil our obligations as a party to a contract for the sale of products or services. It is possible we use this data periodically to send you important messages, such as notifications about orders.

Personal data protection

The administrator takes precautionary measures, including administrative, technical and physical measures, to protect your data from loss, theft or misuse, as well as unauthorised access, change or deletion.

When you publish personal data to forums, chat rooms or social media services, the personal data being shared is visible to other users and can be read, collected or used by them. In this case you have the sole responsibility for the personal data you choose to share.

We keep your personal data for a period of time necessary for the completion of the particular objective, for which the same is being processed, after which it will be deleted, unless a law or a legal regulation necessitates that it be kept for a longer period.

Making your data available

In some cases HRANMASH can make certain personal data available to strategic partners – subcontractors who work with us by providing products or services connected with our business. In the cases where HRANMASH shares information with third parties – contractors of HRANMASH, the company has mechanisms with which to guarantee that they provide a level of data protection in accordance to the relevant agreed upon standards. Your data is considered sensitive for our partners as well.

It is possible that – due to a law, a legal process, a court trial and/or by request from a public or governmental organisations – HRANMASH would be required to make your personal data available. It is possible that we make your information available if we consider this to be necessary or appropriate for the purposes of national security, the application of laws or other issues of public importance.

Rights of the personal data holder

If you wish to withdraw your agreement for personal data processing, make a correction, to object to its processing or to request that your data be deleted (“the right to be forgotten”), you should send a request pertaining to this to info@hranmash-bg.com. . In order to avoid misuse, we reserve the right to ask for additional information about the identity or the person sending the request and the individual concerned.

Personal data request shall be replied to within one month, and in cases where an extension of this period is necessary you will be notified about this extension, as well as the reasons for the delay. We can decline to process request which are repeated without merit or which endanger the privacy of other users. In all cases, you have the right to send a complaint to the Commission for personal data protection, kzld@cpdp.bg, Sofia 1592, 2 Prof. Tsvetan Lazarov Blvd.

Validity and updates of this policy

Our Personal data protection policy can be updated periodically. After updating the current policy we will place a notification and the updated policy on our website.
